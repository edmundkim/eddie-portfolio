# Speaking Image Ranking

This ranking is based on what works best for a portfolio `Speaking` section, not just which photo is nicest on its own.

## What I optimized for

- Eddie is clearly identifiable.
- The image reads as speaking, panel participation, or facilitation right away.
- The event context supports the AI, design, and leadership story.
- The composition is flexible enough for future desktop and mobile crops.
- Lighting, focus, and overall polish feel strong enough for a portfolio homepage.

## Best overall pick

If you use only one image, use image 6: `uxdx-berlin-sap-panel-close.jpg`.

Why it works best:

- Eddie is the visual focal point and clearly in mid-contribution.
- The audience and panel setting add credibility without overpowering him.
- The event screen reinforces the topic and makes the image feel current and relevant.
- The landscape framing gives us the most flexibility later for responsive layouts.
