## What this project proves
- Can shape a native mobile product in a complex hardware, service, and partner ecosystem.
- Can combine research, IA, flows, prioritization, and iterative testing in a high-ambiguity EV product space.

- The case study leans on process breadth and role listing more than specific product decisions and their effects.
- Some of the strongest proof appears trapped in images and prototypes, while the written narrative falls back on generic portfolio language.

## Overlap with current portfolio

## Recommended placement
