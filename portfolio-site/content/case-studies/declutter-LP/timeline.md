## Key turning points

- Q2 2024 research reframed the problem as list-page decision friction and weak hierarchy, not just visual clutter.
- The Q2 2024 formative mWeb test gave evidence to keep pushing a more reduced direction rather than a conservative cleanup.
- Oct 2024 experiments showed that stronger declutter could improve key funnel signals, making the project strategically meaningful.
- The Feb 2025 partner-links phase exposed finance / private-lead / Check24 tradeoffs and prevented a simple immediate rollout.
- By Sep–Oct 2025, the programme had converged on platform-specific answers rather than one universal card, even though recommendation material was still not perfectly clean.
